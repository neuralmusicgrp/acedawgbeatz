Artist Bio

Anthony "Acedawg" Boyd

Singer, songwriter, producer, engineer, and publisher. Acedawg has carved his own lane in music since 2000, building records, commercial music, and independent releases from the ground up.

He released Acedawg Productions Vol. 1 as an independently distributed project through MP3.com and D.A.M. CD distribution, helping establish an early worldwide online pathway for independent music. He later composed the A Step In Time Chimney Sweeps commercial, which went on to win the 2008 Best Commercial in Virginia.

Origin Story

Independent before it was fashionable

Anthony “Acedawg” Boyd’s lane was never built by waiting for permission. He was already engineering, recording, writing, and shaping records in Virginia while studying the craft from the inside out. By 2000, he released Acedawg Productions Vol. 1, a collection of songs recorded with friends locally and distributed online worldwide through MP3.com and D.A.M. CDs — a major independent move for that era. fileciteturn6file4

That early move matters. It shows a producer who understood the future before the crowd caught up — recording locally, publishing independently, and distributing globally.

“This is not random beat posting. This is catalog building, sound design, songwriting, production, and publishing with long-term replay value.”
